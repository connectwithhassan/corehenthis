About Coherent HIS

Coherent HIS is your complete clinic management solution, combining Electronic Medical Records (EMR), Enterprise Practice Management (EPM), and Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) in one intelligent platform. Built for the Middle East, Coherent simplifies clinical workflows, captures comprehensive patient records, and streamlines revenue cycles—while meeting the strictest Department of Health (DOH), Dubai Health Authority (DHA) and Ministry of Health (MOH) compliance requirements.

With advanced features such as AI-Powered Clinical Documentation, integrated laboratory and radiology workflows, medication and allergy management, and robust billing and claims processing, Coherent helps clinics deliver accurate, efficient, and patient-centered care. Secure, intuitive, and scalable, Coherent empowers clinics to enhance patient outcomes, reduce administrative burden, and achieve sustainable growth with confidence.
