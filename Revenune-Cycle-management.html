Revenue Cycle Management

Coherent's Revenue Cycle Management module is designed to help healthcare providers manage their billing and reimbursement processes more efficiently.

Claims Management:

Coherent’s RCM module includes a claims management feature that allows billing personnel to create and submit claims electronically. This helps to reduce errors and accelerate the claims submission process, resulting in faster reimbursement.

Prior Authorization:

Our RCM module includes a prior authorization feature that automates the process of obtaining prior authorization from payers for certain services and procedures. This helps to reduce delays in care and ensures that providers are reimbursed for the services they provide.

Remittance:

Coherent’s RCM module includes a remittance feature that helps healthcare providers track payments and reconcile accounts. This allows providers to easily identify and resolve any discrepancies in payments, ensuring that they are reimbursed accurately and in a timely manner.

Integration with DHA and DOH (UAE region):

Coherent’s RCM module is integrated with DHA and DOH, allowing healthcare providers to submit claims, obtain prior authorization, and receive remittance advice electronically. This integration streamlines the billing and reimbursement process, reducing administrative burden and improving efficiency.

Benefits

  • Increased Efficiency: RCM module helps healthcare providers work more efficiently by automating many of the billing and reimbursement processes.
  • Faster Reimbursement: With electronic claims submission and real-time tracking of payments, Coherent's RCM module helps healthcare providers get paid faster.
  • Improved Accuracy: By eliminating manual processes and reducing the risk of errors, Coherent's RCM module helps to ensure that providers are reimbursed accurately and in a timely manner.
  • Compliance: Our RCM module is designed to be compliant with local regulations, including those of DHA and DOH, ensuring that providers are meeting regulatory requirements.
  • Seamless Integration: With integration with DHA and DOH, RCM module allows healthcare providers to easily submit claims, obtain prior authorization, and receive remittance advice electronically, streamlining the billing and reimbursement process.
