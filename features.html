Smart Features for Smarter Clinics

Coherent HIS offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to streamline clinic management, enhance patient care, and optimize operational efficiency. From AI-powered clinical documentation to integrated billing and claims processing, our platform empowers healthcare providers to deliver exceptional service with ease and confidence.

Scheduling

Appointment management and patient scheduling

Compliance

Compliance with Dubai Health Authority (DHA) and Department of Health (DOH – Abu Dhabi) regulations, including data privacy and security.

EMR

The EMR module of Coherent allows healthcare providers to easily create, edit, and access patient medical records.

Interface


Appointment management and patient schedulingThe system includes a user-friendly interface that allows healthcare providers to easily navigate through patient records, view clinical notes, lab results, radiology reports, and medication history.

Analytics

Reports and analytics to measure clinical and financial performance.

Billing

Automated billing and revenue cycle management.

Integration

The billing module of Coherent is fully integrated with the EMR module and automates the process of generating and submitting claims to insurance providers.

Navigation

The system includes a user-friendly interface that allows billers and finance personnel to easily navigate through patient billing information, view claims status, and generate reports.

Performance

The billing module also includes a built-in revenue cycle management system that helps healthcare providers to manage their financial performance more effectively.
