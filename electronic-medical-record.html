Electronic Medical Record


The Electronic Medical Record (EMR) module in Coherent HIS is designed to help healthcare providers create, manage, and modify patient records with ease.

Clinical module

  • Ability to create, edit, and store comprehensive patient’s clinical information
  • Track and manage patient medical history, surgical history, family and social history, allergies, medications, immunizations and more
  • Intuitive design and navigation for easy access to patient information

Charting and Documentation

  • Streamlined charting and documentation process for recording patient data and progress notes
  • Integration of templates for various specialties and patient visits
  • Pre-designed templates for General Consultation, Surgeries, Radiology, Family Medicine, Obstetrics & Gynecology and more

Clinical Decision Support

  • Built-in clinical decision support tools that aid in diagnosis, treatment planning, and medication management
  • Real-time tracking of patient vital signs and test results
  • Alerts and Reminders:

Alerts and Reminders



Coherent HIS’s EMR module includes automated alerts and reminders that help healthcare providers stay on top of important clinical information. These alerts can be triggered by a variety of factors, such as abnormal lab results, overdue preventive screenings, or potential drug interactions.

Evidence-Based Guidelines



Our Clinical Decision Support feature is built on evidence-based guidelines and clinical best practices. This ensures that healthcare providers have access to the most up-to-date and accurate information when making decisions about patient care.

Clinical Protocols



Coherent’s EMR module includes customizable clinical protocols that guide healthcare providers through the diagnostic and treatment process. These protocols are based on clinical best practices and can be customized to meet the needs of different specialties and patient populations.

Ordering and Results Management

Coherent’s Orders and Results module streamlines the process of ordering and tracking tests, procedures, and medications. Here are some key features






Electronic Orders:

Orders and Results module allows healthcare providers to create electronic orders for tests, procedures, and medications. This eliminates the need for paper-based orders and ensures that orders are legible and accurate.




Order Tracking:

Our Orders and Results module includes a tracking feature that allows healthcare providers to monitor the status of orders in real-time. This helps to ensure that orders are completed in a timely manner and that patients receive the care they need.




Result Management:

Orders and Results module includes a result management feature that allows healthcare providers to view and manage test results in real-time. This includes the ability to flag abnormal results for further review and follow-up.






Interoperability:

Our Orders and Results module is designed to be interoperable with other healthcare systems, allowing for seamless data exchange and collaboration across different care settings.






Medication Management:

Orders and Results module includes a medication management feature that allows healthcare providers to electronically prescribe and manage medications.






Referrals:

Internal and external referrals for quick and seamless management of referral orders where doctors can route the patient to other consultants.

Interoperability and Data Sharing:

  • Interoperability with other healthcare systems for seamless data exchange
  • Integration with national health information exchange networks for secure data sharing
  • Ability to send and receive electronic referrals, consults, and orders
  • UAE based integration with Malaffi, NABIDH and Riayati platforms through HL7 standard

Benefits:

  • Improved patient care: With comprehensive patient records and clinical decision support tools, Coherent HIS’s EMR module helps you make informed decisions about patient care.
  • Increased efficiency: Streamlined charting and documentation processes, customizable templates, and voice recognition and dictation options help you complete documentation more quickly and efficiently.
  • Seamless data exchange: Interoperability with other healthcare systems and medical devices, as well as integration with health information exchange networks, enables seamless data exchange and communication.
  • Flexibility: Coherent HIS’s EMR module can be customized to meet the needs of different specialties and patient populations.
